Where the data comes from

1

Badar creates the signal

No AI, no fetching. Badar types entry, TP and SL in the composer. The platform timestamps it, locks it, and broadcasts it. The trading brain is Badar, always.

Source: Badar himself · Time: whenever he trades
2

Delivered to WhatsApp in seconds

One tap sends the branded signal card to every connected group through the linked WhatsApp number. Subscribers see it instantly, formatted, never lost in chat noise.

Source: connected WhatsApp number · Time: within seconds of publish
3

Live prices grade every signal

From the moment of publish, the Proof Engine watches a live market price feed tick by tick. When price crosses TP or SL, the result is recorded with the exact date and time. Nobody can edit it, including Badar.

Source: market data feed (e.g. TwelveData / MT5 stream) · Time: real-time, 24/5
4

Broker IB report feeds the Revenue Radar

The broker's IB portal reports which referred accounts traded and what rebates were earned. V1: daily CSV upload from the portal. V1.5: automatic sync via broker API. The platform matches accounts to group members.

Source: broker IB portal · Time: synced once daily

What is automated vs. what stays Badar

⚙️ Automated (one-way broadcasts only)

  • Signal card formatting and delivery
  • Result grading and track-record updates
  • Re-engagement nudges, written once by Badar in his own words, personalized per member, sent at randomized human hours
  • Group joins/removals based on IB status

🧑 Always Badar (never automated)

  • Every trading decision and every signal
  • All one-to-one conversations with subscribers
  • All replies inside the groups
  • Voice notes, market commentary, mentoring
The platform never chats on Badar's behalf. Subscribers can't "catch an AI" because a human sends every conversational message. Broadcasts are in Badar's own words, from his templates.
